About the Rossmann Store Sales Dataset

Built around a real retail forecasting benchmark with strong business relevance.

🏪

What is Rossmann?

Rossmann is one of Europe’s largest drugstore retail chains. The dataset was released as part of a Kaggle competition for forecasting daily sales.

📊

Dataset Overview

DatasetRossmann Store Sales
Stores1115
Historical Records~844,000
Training PeriodJan 2013 – Jul 2015

Features Used

Store Promo Promo2 School Holiday State Holiday Competition Distance Competition Duration Promo Duration Store Type Assortment Season Calendar Features
🔮

Why can the model predict future dates?

The original dataset contains historical sales only through 2015. This application creates engineered calendar and promotional features and applies historical relationships to estimate future performance.

Model limitations

Forecasting is strategic support, not a guaranteed outcome.

⚠️

Model Limitations

The model is trained on historical Rossmann behavior. External influences such as inflation, policy changes, and major economic events are not included. Forecast accuracy depends on how similar future conditions are to past retail patterns.
